This webpage is a mini checklist with some resources to help you get on with your personal tutoring. For suggestions, changes, corrections, or dead links, contact me at m.oustamanolakis@mmu.ac.uk or find me on Teams

Please note: The checkboxes may reset when you refresh the page and aren't saved. They are there to indicate this is a task that you can do and offer you the satisfaction of ticking off a checkbox. No data is collected, and nobody would know if you ticked the boxes or not.

Invitation to Week 2 meeting
Congratulations!

Congratulations on reaching the end of your 1st week of Multimedia Journalism! How are you feeling?

I've scheduled our next Personal Tutoring meeting for X. Please 'accept' the meeting and use the Teams link below to join. If you're unable to attend, please let me know and suggest an alternative time in the same week. It's important that we have this regular contact.

If I don't admit you to the meeting straight away, it's because my previous meeting has overrun slightly. Please be patient and I'll come to you as soon as I can.

In advance of our meeting, I'd like you to spend some time on a Self-Assessment Activity. Please rate yourself (1 to 10 with 10 being the strongest) in the following areas:

  • Collaboration - working in a team with other people, participating in classroom activities and discussions
  • Creativity - trying new things even when they’re challenging and learning from the experience
  • Professionalism - writing accuracy (spelling, grammar, punctuation)/digital skills (delete as necessary)
  • Self-Motivation - engaging with your studies (completing the preparation activities, attending timetabled classes), time management and self-organisation
  • Social Awareness - getting involved in the university community (eg student societies), RISE opportunities, volunteering

This isn't a formal test! It's just an opportunity for you to reflect and for us to discuss how to get the most out of your course. So please fill in your scores on the template I’ve attached [Download https://stummuac-my.sharepoint.com/:w:/g/personal/55115502_ad_mmu_ac_uk/EUFeTn9dTu9Bv84TL7V_3pkBhzCIO0EzqPGn-FC0wq5svQ?e=wsEqjT] and upload it to My Career Hub. It’ll tell you how to send a link to me so that we can chat about it at next week’s meeting. We can also use the time to talk through any questions or concerns you might have.

Take care

(Your name)

Week 6 – Email
Hello,

Well, we’ve reached the end of the first Block of teaching and next week is Assessment Week.

You can find all the details about your assessment on Moodle. Just go to your Block 1 unit and click on the Assessment information area. The Assessment brief will explain the task. You can also find your submission deadline on Moodle.

If you have any concerns or questions about the assessment, please don’t suffer in silence! Your tutors are here to support you. You can contact your unit tutors or you can drop me a line and we’ll get things sorted.

Other sources of help are Study Skills Tutors. And you might find it helpful to check out our Wellbeing resources.

Good luck!
