This webpage is a mini list with some resources to help you get on with your personal tutoring. For suggestions, changes, corrections, or dead links, contact me at m.oustamanolakis@mmu.ac.uk or find me on Teams

Please note: The checkboxes may reset when you refresh the page and aren't saved. They are there to indicate this is a task that you can do and offer you the satisfaction of ticking off a checkbox. No data is collected, and nobody would know if you ticked the boxes or not.

In block 1

Email - Suggested script to schedule 2nd meeting tutees
Hello student name!

Either

I am your name, and I am going to be your personal tutor this year.

or

Welcome to the second year of your studies! I will continue being your personal tutor

We have planned some great resources for you, and I will guide you through this year, to discuss about your career and future plans.

Before we meet, can you please read our page about Graduate Attributes and try to evaluate yourself, how do you think you -personaly- do in those 5 attributes? Don't worry, this is not a formal assessment, it's just a conversation started between us. Could you please write your impressions on a document and upload it here in the self-assessment? We can talk about it on our meeting

Can you mark down on your calendar date and time and meet me at location with puppies?

Take care,
(Your name)

In block 2

Email - Suggested script to schedule 2nd meeting tutees
Hello student name!

I hope you're doing well. It was nice to see you on during block 1. I'd like to meet you again in block 2 or a date that works for you and have a chat.

Before our meeting, can you complete the one of the following activities?

One activity is to go to our Career Pulse website. There are 14 questions about self-awareness, employability skills, career possibilities and work experience. It's a lot, but you don't have to do all 14. Please focus on area(s) that you consider most suitable for your tutee, and we'll have a chat about it.

Or another activity that you can do before we meet, is to explore the various carreer pathways that you can take. We can then discuss it further when we meet.

I look forward to meeting you again!

(Your name)
